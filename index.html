ABOUT ANTARA

The story of a real black hero.

Antara is an independently financed, 5th Century Swords and Sandals Epic which is to be directed by Tarsem Singh. It tells the story of Antara Ibn Shaddad, a black slave who won his freedom and became a knight, and one of the most famous poets pre-Islamic Arabia. Antara became an icon of Arabic literature and a monument for the warrior code of ancient Arabia. His poetry was sewn in gold and suspended inside the Kaaba in Mecca before the days of Islam. If one were to study Arabic literature in Oxford or Cambridge, they would study the literary works of Antara.

The story of Antara is both the Alexander the Great and Romeo and Juliet of the Middle East and has all the elements of a classical, timeless, and Shakespearean epic. The production comes at a time when the world is ready to see the story of a true black hero, which until now, has been missed by Hollywood.

SHOOTING LOCATION

Antara will be shot in the NEOM desert, Saudi Arabia which has some of the most spectacular, never before seen locations the world has to offer. NEOM is an ancient desert with incredible ruins which date back to the Nabetian civilisation, which shared a trading route with Petra, Jordan. The same architecture and carvings as can be seen in Petra can also be seen in NEOM.

NFT

Antara is the first movie in history funded by an NFT, the Arabian Camels. The Arabian Camels were one of the most iconic NFT drops of 2021. The Arabian Camels helped to launch the Antara IP, and offered a never before seen utility that allows NFT holders to go on set, get parts in the movie, and get tickets to the premier etc.
